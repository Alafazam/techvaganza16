ABHIVYAKTI

"Poetry is when an emotion has found its thought and the thought has found words" -Robert Frost

There are two separate Events :-

  • Poetry:

There's a poet in everyone. See if your words can resonate with the feelings of the world.

  • Creative writing:

Wit, Logic, Innovation, Sarcasm and a way with Words. Do you pride yourself on having these abilities. Test out your mettle against those who think they got it more than you.

Events details:

Problem Statement:

  • Poetry:

"HOPE"/"UMMEED" (Amidst any Scenario/Incidence You can think of).

  • Creative writing:

The theme shall be given at the spot during the event (visual/audio input).

Event format:

  • Poetry Competition:

Online/Offline submission on the given Theme.

  • Creative writing:

Short Story based on some input.

Important Dates:

The entry should be submitted latest by 11 am on 23rd May, 2015.

Rules:

Poetry:

  • The submissions can be made online/offline as per your convenience.
  • The theme of the event is provided.
  • The online submissions are to be emailed at abhivyakti@techvaganza.org with the subject POETRY .
  • Language is no bar ( But the script should be in Hindi/English).
  • For the offline submission, students shall submit a PRINTED COPY at the Humanities department.
  • The submissions should reach by 11 am on 23rd may
  • Plagiarism shall lead to disqualification.

Creative writing:

  • An input shall be provided to students.
  • The students will have to write a short, innovative story based on the theme.
  • The Time Limit is 1 hour.

Judging criteria:

Poetry/Creative writing:

The submissions shall be judged by a panel of judges.

Arena:

Hi-Tech Room,Online/Offline Submission.
