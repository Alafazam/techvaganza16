Event format:

Round 1:

This is a pen - and - paper puzzle round where the teams are supposed to solve Mathematical puzzles in the given time. The top 20-25% teams will be taken to 2nd round.

Round 2:

This is a Rapid fire round. Each team will be given only 60 seconds time and the number of questions answered by that team in that time will be counted. The top 7 teams will be finalized in this round.

Round 3:

In this round, the questions will be on 'General Knowledge' of Mathematics, Mathematicians etc.

Note:

This event can be participated by a team of two of individually. The event comprises of 3 rounds

Judgement Criteria

  • Round1: Points based on their performance in quiz
  • Round2: This is the semi-final round.points in the 1st round and this round will be summed up.
  • Round3: This is the final round. Based on points from previous stages, 7 teams will be selected. Team will have to compete with other teams to win. In this round, mathematical knowledge of each participant will be tested. And who perform better within time will be winners. .
