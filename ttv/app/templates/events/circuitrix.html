CIRCUITRIX

The legacy continues as the circuit design challenge "circuitrix" comes to the valley. So be a part of it and show your ability to design at the level where it matters...

Rounds:

Round 1:

This will be an objective- type question answer round. The question paper will include basic questions related to electrical circuits, analog and digital electronics. However a separate section will include questions related to aptitude as well.

Round 2:

This will be a circuit designing round where the filtered participants will be provided with the names of the five circuits they have to design in the specified time. Since the name of the event is "circuitrix" trick is still missing! And the participants will get to know of that on the day of the event.

Rules & Judging Criteria:

  • Only Ten teams with maximum scores in first round will be selected for the next round.
  • The scoring pattern for second round will be given on the day of the event .The team with the maximum score will win this challenge.
  • Time limit will be given at the venue for each round .
  • Participation can be either in a group of maximum 2 members or an individual.
