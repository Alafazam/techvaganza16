QUADCOPTERS

HIGHLIGHTS OF WORKSHOP :

  • Lectures on Quadcopter modelling,working techniques, working theories, design considerations, overview of flying a quadcopter, latest technologies in robotics, different systems in an automobile, R&D growth and employment aspects in these industries.
  • It will provide hands-on training of assembling of Quadcopter kit, modelling of quadcopter and their working.
  • Flying sessions of quadcopters
  • Quiz and Competition

EVENT DETAILS : WORKSHOP CONTENTS

Embedded Systems Design, Introduction to controllers and embedded systems design, Working principle, Constructional concepts, Quadcopter frames, Materials used, Designing concepts, Hands on designing, Developing of quad frame models, Types of frame structures and consequences , 2.4 Ghz radio communication, Radio transmitter, Radio receiver, Transmitter controls, Controls corresponding to stabilisation techniques, Power limitations, Types of power sources, Frequency selection , Antenna arrangements, Coverage consideration , Orientation of quad in terms of coverage distance, Number of channels, Effective utilisation of channels, Connection consideration of various configurations, Consequences of reception beyond coverage area, Multi rotor stabilisation controller, Consideration and concepts of stability, Component explanation of the board, Operations of gyroscopes, Microcontroller operations, Variations in output of ESC, Gain potentiometers, ISP flashing techniques, Electronic speed controllers, Speed control techniques, Ampere ratings of ESC ,Input supply considerations, Data Signaling to ESC, Battery elimination operation of ESCs , Choosing right ESCs for the right application, Constructional ideas of building your own ESC ,Programming ESC, BLDC Motors, Wide varieties of motors, Difference of a Normal DC motor and BLDC motor, Applications of BLDC motors, Advantages of BLDC motors, Driving a BLDC motor, Characters, Commutation, Analysis & Evaluation methods, Speed calculation, Propellers, Construction of Propellers, Types of propellers, Suitable propeller for right application,Parts of a propeller, Construction of Quadcopter, Components Identification, Complete Assembling, Simulation of flight in FMS, Flying precautions, Flying Session.

Quadcopter kit will be provided only during the duration of the workshop for training purpose.It won't be given to the students permanently.

However, if the students wish to get the kit permanently , they may do so by purchasing it at reduced prizes.For that extra charges other than the registration fees have to be paid.

This workshop can be undertaken by students of all branches and would be highly beneficial for students from the disciplines of Mechanical engineering, Electrical engineering and Electronics engineering.

Quiz will be conducted at the end of the interactive sessions.

Hurry up. Limited seats available.

COMPETITION

There will be a competition based on modelling and assembling a quadcopter.

JUDGING CRITERIA

Faculty from mechanical department along with members of Codex Design Services will judge the competition.The results will depend on the following things

  • Structural model and assembling of parts of the quadcopter..
  • Stability of flight of the quadcopter
  • Duration of flight of the quadcopter
