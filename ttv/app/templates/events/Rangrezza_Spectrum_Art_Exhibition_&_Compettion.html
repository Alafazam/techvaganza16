Rangrezza Spectrum

Event format:

Round1(Day 1, 2, 3 ):

Exhibition of self-created participant's handmade art pieces.

Round2(Day 2):

On the spot painting Competition.

Day 3:

Result

Rules:

  • Participant's works may be of the following types:-
    • Sketching
    • Drawing/ Coloring
    • Oil Painting/Acrylic Painting
    • Water Painting
    • Glass Painting etc.
  • Entries may be submitted on paper/canvas/glass etc.
  • The deadline for submission of all the painting to the organizers at NIT SRINAGAR would be 21st May, 2015 (Thursday) which will be strictly followed.
  • Digital art not allowed.
  • There is no restriction on age group.
  • No theme in particular. Contestants are free to create whatever they like.
  • There are no restrictions on the number of entries submitted by a participant.
  • Please note that this is not an online painting competition. The painting/artwork should be physically present or must reach the organizers by the deadline mentioned.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • No adult themed paintings will be accepted.
  • Each work should be the original creation of the participant i.e., the painting must be an unpublished work.
  • The participant will be required to show the proof that it is his/her art work.
    (Example a photograph of them with their painting, photograph of the painting when it was partially complete etc.)
  • Best Paintings/artwork will be published in the Exhibition.
  • All the painting/artwork should be submitted with participant's name, Title for the artwork along with Category and Description.
  • The best painting/artwork will be published on the Techvaganza 2015 Facebook Page on 22nd May 2015 (Friday).

Judging Criteria:

The judges shall Judge the entries from among all eligible entries based on the following criteria:-
  • Creativity and originality of the depicted theme.
  • Visual Impact.
  • Quality of artistic composition and overall design.

Winning Criteria:

  • 60% will be based on the decision of Judges.
  • 20% will be based on the number of Facebook likes the participant can get to their painting link (which will be provided to them once their painting gets selected for publishing).
  • 20% will be based on the spot voting by the visitors.

ON THE SPOT THEME PAINTING COMPETITION:

For Kids (5years - 14years)
(2nd day - 23rd May 2015)

  • The painting theme/topic will be declared on the spot or a day before the competition
  • Chart paper will be provided on the competition day .
  • Colors will be provided.
  • Time constraint: 2-3 hours.
  • Exciting prizes for the winners of "on the spot painting".
  • The participants can bring their own colors and brushes.
  • The winning paintings will also be displayed in the exhibition as well.

Note:

The winners of "Exhibition" are different from those of "On the spot painting".
